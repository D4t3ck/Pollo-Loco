El Pollo Loco

Story

Welcome to Mexico amigo!

In a small Mexican village named "Pollopia," known for its delicious chicken dishes, unrest reigns. The chickens, once loyal companions to humans, have risen against their former masters. But that's not the worst – they follow a gigantic, mutated chicken, larger than all others. This monster chicken has taken control and plans to subjugate humanity.

In this time of crisis, Pepe Peligroso steps up. A brave Mexican with a sharp mind and an even sharper mustache. The government has tasked him with combating the threat posed by the rebellious chickens and saving humanity.

Armed with his courage and his favorite weapon, a bottle of spicy salsa sauce, Pepe sets out. He traverses dangerous terrain, from the dusty streets of the village to the dark forests where the chickens lurk.

With skillful jumps and swift movements, Pepe navigates through the levels, collecting salsa sauce and gold coins, and battling the hordes of chickens. Finally, he reaches the climax of his adventure – an epic showdown against the gigantic chicken.
